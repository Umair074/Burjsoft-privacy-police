Privacy Policy for Drawing App

Last Updated: 07-May-2026

1. Introduction

At Burjsoft ("we", "our", or "us"), protecting your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our drawing and creativity mobile application ("App").

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

Our App is designed to provide a simple and creative drawing experience. We may collect limited information to improve app functionality and user experience.

a. Information Collected Automatically:

When you use the App, certain information may be collected automatically, including:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Language settings
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • General usage analytics

This information is collected in a non-personal and aggregated manner whenever possible.

b. Drawing Content:

The drawings, sketches, or images you create within the App are stored locally on your device unless you choose to share or back them up using third-party services available on your device.

We do not access, collect, or store your personal drawings on our servers.

3. Advertising

The App may display advertisements provided by third-party advertising networks. These services may use device identifiers and limited usage information to provide and improve ads.

Third-party ad providers may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

4. Analytics

We may use third-party analytics tools to understand how users interact with the App, improve features, and fix technical issues.

Analytics services may collect:

  • Device information
  • Session duration
  • Feature usage
  • Crash diagnostics

This data does not personally identify individual users.

5. Permissions

The App may request certain permissions depending on the features you use, such as:

  • Storage/Photos Access — to save and load drawings
  • Camera Access (optional) — to import photos for drawing or coloring
  • Microphone Access (if applicable) — for specific creative features

Permissions are only used for the functionality described and can be managed through your device settings.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information to third parties.

We may share limited data with:

  • Analytics providers
  • Advertising partners
  • Service providers that help operate the App

These parties are required to handle data securely and only for authorized purposes.

Our App is intended for general audiences and may be used for adults due to advertising. The ads-free PRO version can be reliable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from you without appropriate legal compliance.

7. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to help protect information and maintain app security. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

8. Third-Party Services

The App may include links or integrations with third-party services such as:

  • Advertising networks
  • Analytics providers
  • Cloud backup or sharing services

These third parties operate under their own privacy policies and practices.

9. Your Choices

You can:

  • Disable permissions through your device settings
  • Limit ad tracking through your device privacy settings
  • Uninstall the App at any time
